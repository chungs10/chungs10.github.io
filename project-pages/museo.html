Museo

This is a class project for Web Science System Development with the theme of creating a website that would make money/create interest in a certain product or brand. Our project's goal is to generate interest in museums and entice future guests to visit them.

The website would have us coordinate with museums by asking them if they can put QR codes that they scan for digital rewards at certain locations in their museums. By using our web application, they would be able to obtain digital rewards from the QR code and then display it on their personal stampbook online. By giving incentives via these stamps, we would be able to encourage more visits to museums and to explore more diverse museums farther away.
