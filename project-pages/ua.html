United Airlines Cargo Weight and Balance Evaluation

This project involved developing a proof of concept web application for United Airlines to evaluate the safety of cargo at the beginning of the trip. The goal was to ensure that cargo placement would comply with safety regulations, optimize aircraft performance, and reduce cargo damage.

I worked closely with three other team members, where I played a significant role in designing and developing the frontend interface. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I ensured that the user experience was intuitive and responsive. I also collaborated on the backend using Python, where I helped integrate the python code into the frontend that allowed us to detect fire supression lines.

The project was a great opportunity to combine various technologies to meet real-world aviation needs. We applied a full-stack approach, ensuring smooth interactions between frontend design and backend functionality.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python.
